PicoGolf

What is PicoGolf?

PicoGolf is a miniature golf game.

The rules of standard PicoGolf

  • The aim is to get the ball from a certain location into a certain hole, with the lowest amount of strikes of a club.
  • Only a club should be used to move the ball during a game.
  • Games are ended by rule-breaking, or by the ball reaching the hole.
  • A PicoGolf ball must have a circumference of ~45mm.
